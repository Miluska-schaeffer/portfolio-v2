Coming from my passion for design and technology, I made the big jump and started my journey learning how to code by myself. After learning the basics of front-end, I decided it was time to deepen my skills and enrolled in a Web Development Bootcamp at Le Wagon Berlin.

While creating real applications on Ruby on Rails, we also had a glance at how User Experience and User Interface Design are key to the product creation process. It was from this experience that I was amazed by the importance of good User Research to create usable and desirable products, that not only work well but give the user a pleasant experience as a whole.

With that in mind and convinced of becoming a UX Designer, I enrolled in the IronHack UX/UI Design Bootcamp, where every week I got to work on different projects following the Design Thinking Methodology and Agile Framework to create efficient and enjoyable user-centered designs.

I am always up for collaboration or just a nice chat.
